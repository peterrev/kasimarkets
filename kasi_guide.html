What is a prediction market?

Kasi Markets is not a casino and it is not a sportsbook. It is something simpler and more powerful.

A prediction market poses a question about a real-world outcome. You take a YES or NO position. If you are right, you win. If you are wrong, you lose your stake. The price of a YES share at any moment reflects the crowd's collective view of how likely that outcome is.

The platform earns a flat fee on every market regardless of outcome. You are never betting against a house — you are betting against other people who disagree with you.

Key difference from betting: Markets cover anything — politics, economics, sport, culture, weather, celebrity. Prices shift in real time. And being right is public, provable, and shareable.
2

How prices work

Every market has a YES price and a NO price. They always add up to R1.00.

YES
57¢
The crowd says 57% chance
Buy at 57¢ → win R1.00 if YES
NO
43¢
The crowd says 43% chance
Buy at 43¢ → win R1.00 if NO

If you think the crowd is wrong, you have an edge. Buy YES at 57¢ when you believe the true probability is higher — and if you are right, you profit the difference.

Think of it like this: If you buy 100 YES shares at 57¢, you spend R57. If the outcome is YES, you receive R100. Your profit is R43. If the outcome is NO, you lose your R57 stake.

Reading the chart

The probability chart shows how the crowd's view has shifted over time.

Probability chart — YES price over 30 days
  • Green line rising = the crowd thinks YES is becoming more likely. The event is looking more probable.
  • Green line falling = the crowd is shifting towards NO. New information is changing the consensus.

How the X sharing flywheel works

1. YOU BET
Place your call
Take a YES or NO position on any market. A shareable card is generated instantly with your position, stake, and potential payout.
2. YOU SHARE
Post to X
Hit "Share to X" — your card goes out to your followers. It shows your conviction, your stake, and what you stand to win. This is a public flex.
3. THEY ENGAGE
Debate erupts
Your followers agree or disagree. Quote-tweets, replies, and threads generate organic reach. The debate drives new users to the market to take the opposite side.
4. RECEIPTS
Winners claim
When the market resolves, winners come back to claim their receipts publicly. Losers get ratio'd. This is the viral moment — and it drives the next wave of sign-ups.
Why this matters commercially: Polymarket's single biggest growth mechanic was organic sharing on X. Users shared open positions, generated debate, and drove new sign-ups with zero paid media. Kasi Markets is built around this exact loop — but tuned for Black Twitter SA, which is already the most prediction-heavy community on the platform.

Sharing step-by-step

  • After placing a bet, scroll to the "Share your position" card on the market detail page (right sidebar).
  • Preview your card — it shows your username, the market question, your position (YES/NO), your price, and your potential payout.
  • Tap "Share to X" to post directly, or "Copy link" to paste into any conversation, WhatsApp group, or thread.
  • Add your own commentary — your hot take is what drives the engagement. The card provides the proof.
  • On results day, come back and quote-tweet your original post with your winnings. This is the receipt.
Pro tip: The best-performing shares on X have a strong opinion attached. Don't just share the card — add your take. "ANC is cooked in Joburg. Mark my words. R200 on it." That's what gets retweeted.

Deposits & withdrawals

Deposit-to-bet in under 60 seconds. Payouts hit your account instantly.

Friction kills conversion. The entire deposit-to-first-bet journey is designed to be under 60 seconds. Any slower and you lose the impulse that brought the user to the page.

Responsible play

Kasi Markets is designed for entertainment and informed opinion. Play responsibly.

  • Daily limits — set a maximum daily spend that cannot be exceeded. Once hit, betting is paused until the next day.
  • Cool-off periods — take a 24-hour, 7-day, or 30-day break from betting at any time.
  • Self-exclusion — permanently exclude your account from the platform if needed.
  • Age verification — you must be 18+ to use Kasi Markets. ID verification is required during sign-up.
  • Transparent resolution — every market has a named, independent resolution source. No ambiguity, no disputes.
