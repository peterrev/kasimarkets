The sentiment engine
Prices that tell the truth
Traditional polls sample a thousand people who might be lying. Kasi Markets aggregates the real-money conviction of thousands who are putting their rands where their mouth is.
📊
Crowd-sourced probability
Every market price is a real-time aggregate of what participants actually believe. When the ANC's price drops to 31¢ in Joburg, that's the crowd saying "31% chance" — weighted by conviction, not just opinion.
⚡
Faster than news
Markets price in new information within minutes. A viral clip of a politician's gaffe moves the line before any outlet publishes a story. The crowd reacts first because the crowd is already watching.
💰
Skin in the game
Talk is cheap. Betting is not. When someone buys YES at 57¢, they're backing their view with real money. This makes prediction market prices a more honest signal than any poll, survey, or pundit take.
🌎
Tuned for South Africa
Markets are created from the topics we're already debating — ANC coalition maths, PSL title races, Eskom updates, rand forecasts. The content mirrors the community. The prices reflect the community.
