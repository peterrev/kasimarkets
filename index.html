How it works
Four steps. Sixty seconds.
No bookie, no house edge, no complexity. A question is asked. You take a side. If you're right, you win.
01
Pick a market
Elections, sport, the rand, Eskom, culture — every market has a named independent resolution source. No ambiguity.
02
Take Yes or No
The price is the crowd's probability. Buy Yes at 57¢ and you're saying the crowd is underestimating the outcome. Minimum bet: R5.
03
Share your call to X
Every bet generates a shareable card. Post it. Start the debate. Your followers can take the other side — or pile on.
04
Cash the receipts
When the outcome resolves, winners get paid to Capitec or PayShap instantly. Come back and claim your receipts on X.
X Integration
Built on what people
are already saying
Kasi Markets isn't just connected to X — it's built on top of the conversations already happening there. Every market reflects real-time public sentiment. Every price is a live signal of what the crowd actually believes.
  • 📈
    Sentiment-driven pricing. Market prices reflect what thousands of people on X are saying, debating, and predicting — not what bookmakers think. When a politician trends for the wrong reasons, the price moves before the news does.
  • 🕐
    Real-time crowd signals. "Mark my words" and "screenshot this" are already prediction market behaviour. Kasi captures that energy and puts real stakes behind it — turning opinions into positions with verifiable outcomes.
  • 🌱
    Organic growth engine. Every bet generates a shareable card. Every share reaches a targeted audience. Every debate drives new sign-ups. The platform grows through the same viral mechanics that make X conversations spread.
  • 💰
    Receipts culture. X already has a receipts culture — people return to old tweets to prove they were right. Kasi makes that provable and profitable. Winners come back, share their payouts, and the next wave of users arrives.
  • 🌎
    More accurate than polls. People lie to pollsters. They don't lie with their money. When thousands of South Africans put R20 on the line, the resulting price is a more honest signal of what people actually believe than any survey.

The sentiment engine
Prices that tell the truth
Traditional polls sample a thousand people who might be lying. Kasi Markets aggregates the real-money conviction of thousands who are putting their rands where their mouth is.
📊
Crowd-sourced probability
Every market price is a real-time aggregate of what participants actually believe. When the ANC's price drops to 31¢ in Joburg, that's the crowd saying "31% chance" — weighted by conviction, not just opinion.
⚡
Faster than news
Markets price in new information within minutes. A viral clip of a politician's gaffe moves the line before any outlet publishes a story. The crowd reacts first because the crowd is already watching.
